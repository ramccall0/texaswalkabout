What color cowboy boot goes with everything? (Explained)  

Texas Walkabout
April 5, 2022
Updated on April 5, 2022

What color cowboy boots go with everything?

The best color cowboy boots that go with everything is black. Whether you are dressing for work, a fancy occasion, or simply doing chores, black cowboy boots will go with your outfit.

You might want to own more than one pair. One can be a simple black cowboy boot, while the other has intricate designs in black leather. Which pair you choose to wear will depend on how dressy you want to look.

Why black cowboy boots?

Think about your everyday clothes and shoes. When you want a color outfit for any occasion, one piece can be black while the other is any color. Your shoes can also be black. Now your outfit looks totally coordinated.

Now apply this same logic to choosing your cowboy boots. Even if you only wish to own one pair, that pair should be black. Then you can rest easy knowing you will always look terrific. If you only intend to wear them for dressy occasions, select a pair with a nice design.

What color cowboy boots are most versatile?

If this is your first ever pair of cowboy boots, your best bet is to go with tan or a rich brown. At this point you probably will not be wearing them to special events anyhow. You should definitely avoid beige or an odd color boot such as red, yellow, or green.

That said, if you are buying them specifically to wear at a dressy social event, then you want black. Black is the most dressy of all the colors of cowboy boots. If you select a pair with an intricate stitching pattern, you will look terrific.

Can cowboy boots go with everything?

Years ago the answer would have been no. However, society has changed and along with that, fashion rules have changed. Today a fine pair of cowboy boots can be worn with suits or even tuxedos. For women, they can go with pantsuits, skirts, and even dresses.

One thing to keep in mind is to make sure the color of your boots goes with your attire. Ideally, if you want to wear cowboy boots, you should have many different colors. This is so you will have a pair to compliment any color outfit. If you can only afford one or two pairs of boots, choose black, brown, or tan.

Can you wear cowboy boots with skinny jeans?

Yes, in fact this fashion trend is quite popular with men, women, and kids. When you want to make a fashion statement while out running everyday errands, wear this combination. Just make sure the colors can go together.

In the case of skinny jeans, the cowboy boots will always be worn on the outside of the jeans. Trying to fit skinny jeans over the outside of the cowboy boots is plain silly. You will look ridiculous. Wearing your boots properly on the outside of your skinny jeans will make you look sensational.

Do brown cowboy boots go with everything?

Yes, if you already own a nice pair of brown cowboy boots, you are in luck. They can go with pretty much everything. You might see rock stars wearing a stylish pair of them with their onstage attire.

As a sign of the times, you will also notice many men wearing a black or grey suit with brown cowboy boots as well. He appears very handsome no matter what outfit he wears with his brown boots.

What about women and brown cowboy boots?

She will look extremely classy wearing them with a knit top and leggings. Women can also adorn brown cowboy boots with a lace dress or a skating-style dress.

We already discussed how appropriate it is to wear skinny jeans with cowboy boots. We will add that those boots can be any shade of brown. If she tops that off with a faux-fur jacket, she will look amazing!

Can kids wear brown cowboy boots?

Yes, they can, and they need not look silly trying to fit into adult-sized boots. Many makers of cowboy boots create models just for kids. Not only will these boots fit the kids very well, but the designs are more appropriate for their age. Their family and friends are going to be envious and probably want their own cowboy boots.

Final thoughts

We have covered a lot of ground in this article. The main thing for the reader to take away from it is that either black, or brown, or tan, is a great color cowboy boot to have. Of these, if you are only into owning one pair, the black is your best choice. You will never go wrong wearing these.
