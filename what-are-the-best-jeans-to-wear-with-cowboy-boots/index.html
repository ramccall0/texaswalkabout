What are the best jeans to wear with cowboy boots? (Explained)

Texas Walkabout
March 6, 2022
Updated on March 10, 2022

There are a lot of styles of cowboy jeans available out there. Knowing how to find the right fit to match your cowboy boots is crucial. In fact, your cowboy jeans have evolved so much over the years.

 

This means that choosing what works for you should coincide with fashion trends and style rules. Having a style guide to make it easier for you to choose your best pair of cowboy jeans should be a great thing. Keep reading to find out more about the best jeans to wear with your cowboy boots.

What are the best jeans to wear with cowboy boots?

There are various styles of jeans that are most suitable for cowboy boots.

Denim jeans make the best pair of cowboy boots. Since these boots are an effortless style choice, the following are great options to wear them.

  • Skinny jeans
  • Bootcut jeans
  • Cropped jeans
  • Denim shorts
  • Cowboy cut jeans
  • Straight leg jeans

How should jeans fit with cowboy boots?

Generally, the jeans should be long enough to cover the shaft of your cowboy boots while ensuring that your legs are protected.

Ideally, when you’re choosing your jeans, it's essential to ensure that you find the right fit with great length. These should rest approximately half an inch above the heel of your boots. Additionally, make sure that the jeans aren’t tucked into your boots.

Why is the type of jeans important for cowboy boots?

The main reasons are to provide comfort and protection.

The thing is that cowboy boots are made for walking on unstructured terrain. There is a great deal of debris and other foreign objects that can snag on your jeans. Protecting your legs is important.

 

When to consider choosing the right pair of jeans for cowboy boots?

If you’re going to be working on a ranch or riding horses all day.

The fact of the matter is that cowboys tend to do a lot of heavy lifting. Moreover, you’ll spend most of your time with various animals. The jeans that you choose should be well-fitted, durable, and comfortable.

Who should be paying attention to the type of jeans for their boots?

Cowboys should be paying attention to the type of jeans that they choose to wear for their boots.

However, anyone who spends an adequate amount of time outdoors should also consider the types of jeans that they were. If you spend a lot of time in a ranch, you’ll be exposed to a lot of debris. You should buy durable jeans to protect your boots as well as your legs from dirt.

Where can you go to find the best pair of jeans for some cowboy boots?

In most retail shops.

It’s easy for you to find a great pair of cowboy jeans. The most important thing is for you to have an idea of the type of jeans that you want to buy. You can shop for your cowboy jeans online or you can go directly to a shop near you.

Since you have a wide range of fits and styles to choose from, finding the best pair of cowboy jeans should be relatively easy for you. Even if you’re a beginner.

 How much longer should jeans be for cowboy boots?

Long enough to cover the heels of your boots.

Ideally, the longer your jeans, the better they are. When you’re buying cowboy jeans, going for something longer instead of shorter is a great option. In fact, choosing jeans that are at least a quarter to an inch above your boots is what’s recommended.

Should jeans stack on cowboy boots?

Yes, jeans should stack on your cowboy boots.

It’s recommended that you should have a minor stack in the leg of your cowboy jeans. Especially when you choose to wear them with your cowboy boots. This is why getting the right fit for your jeans is significant. Finding jeans that are the right length that can rest just above your heels is what’s ideal.

Do Cowboys wear their pants over their boots?

Not anymore.

Back in the days there were many cowboys who would tuck their pants inside their boots. This was a way to prevent any dirt from forming at the helm of their jeans. In addition, this was a good way to help prevent their jeans from catching any sticks or snagging on the fence.

But, times have changed and more and more cowboys prefer to wear their pants over their boots. Mainly, this helps to give a slight stack when the cowboy jeans fit well.

Can you wear slim jeans with cowboy boots?

 

Yes, you can.

The thing about cowboy jeans is that they come in different styles. Some people prefer to not stack their jeans. Instead, they choose to tuck in their jeans. Slim or skinny jeans are a better option because they can fit better inside if you want to tuck them inside.

In summary, a lot of thought should go into the type of cowboy jeans that you choose. There are various styles that you can choose from. Moreover, the type of lifestyle that you live should also influence what jeans you wear.

When you’re thinking of cowboy jeans that should go with your cowboy boots, you should also think of the length. Even though people do wear cowboy boots with denim shorts, it’s always a great idea to go for a longer length. This not only plays a vital role in protecting your legs, but it also helps you to have a slight stack that’s fashionably recommended.
