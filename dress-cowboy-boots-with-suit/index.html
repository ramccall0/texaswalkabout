7 Tips For Wearing Dress cowboy boots With A Suit

Texas Walkabout
March 13, 2022
Updated on March 13, 2022

Do the fashion mavens think it is okay to wear dress cowboy boots with a suit or dress? That is what we are going to talk about in this concise article. If you care what people think of your attire and accessories, you will want to read this.

Can you wear dress cowboy boots with a suit?

Many years ago the answer to this question would have been absolutely not. But, this is 2022. Society has changed and so has the ever-changing world of fashion. Today it is perfectly acceptable to wear cowboy boots with a man's suit or slacks, a woman's pantsuit, skirt or dress.

Does the style of the boots matter? In a sense, yes. The pair of boots you want to wear depends on the look you are trying to achieve. If you are wearing a casual outfit, the cowboy boots can be casual. If it is a formal occasion, a spiffier boot should be worn. Typically, if you are wearing a dressy suit, it is either for business or formal activities. Therefore, your cowboy boots ought to mirror your suit.

When should you wear dress cowboy boots?

It totally depends on the outfit you are wearing as well as the occasion. Not every style of dress cowboy boots will go with every suit. As we discussed in the previous paragraph, the boots ought to compliment the suit. For example, you would not wear thick winter boots with a lightweight dressy, formal suit.

Another instance would be the reverse. If it is cold outside, and you are wearing a heavy clad suit, then your thick winter boots will be fine. The occasion has something to do with it too. If it is a special occasion, and you are wearing an appropriately-styled suit, then your dress cowboy boots should compliment it. That goes for the color of your attire as well.

What cowboy boots go good with suits?

Ideally you ought to own many pairs of cowboy boots, one pair for every purpose that arises. Some should be dressy, some casual, others good for working or driving in. They also need to come in many colors, including at least one pair in black, one in brown, and one in tan.

That said, once again times have changed. A couple of decades ago, cowboy boots only came in a few designs, colors, and thicknesses. In today's marketplace there have to be literally hundreds of choices. It is up to you to select pairs that you love and are comfortable to wear. Additionally, it is best if you try them on before you buy them. That way you can make sure they fit well.

What about women and dressy cowgirl boots?

The same basic fashion rules apply to women as to men. They can wear dressy cowgirl boots with their business and formal attire. The boots need to compliment the outfit she is wearing. It is feasible to wear dressy boots with pantsuits, short dresses, and short skirt with a top combination.

Are there exceptions? Yes, if she is wearing a long dress or skirt it would probably look silly to wear cowboy boots with that. This is true even if the boots are a dressy pair. In that case, she really needs a different type of footwear. Other than that, it is up to the individual whether to wear dressy boots with her outfit.

Can you wear cowboy boots with slacks?

It is doable, yes. The same basic fashion rules apply here. Match up the appropriate boots with your slacks. The dressier your attire Is, the dressier your boots should be. This applies to men and women, children too if they like to wear cowboy boots.

One thing to make note of is you should NOT tuck your slacks (or suit pants) into your boots. Instead, they ought to be draped down over the boots. You can violate this fashion rule if you wish, but don't be surprised if people mock you.

Are there certain brands and models of dress cowboy boots that you should have?

As we said earlier, these days there are hundreds of boots to select from including dress cowboy boots. The kind you buy is totally up to you. Nobody is going to tell you what to do here. On the other hand, if you are looking for sound advice, we will offer some.

We won't tell you what kind of dress boots to buy, just suggest a strategy for choosing them. You are best off going into a brick-and-mortar store where you can view all the varieties and try them on. You want a pair that is comfortable as well as stylish. Otherwise, they will hurt your feet and wind up on the closet floor most of the time.

Final Thoughts

We can be your go-to source on how to choose a pair of dress cowboy boots. We are western wear fashion experts and will assist you in making your choices. That said, whether you wear dress boots or other superb footwear with your suit is completely up to you. Go ahead, buy them and enjoy them!
