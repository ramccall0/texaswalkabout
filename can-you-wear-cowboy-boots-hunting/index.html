Can you wear cowboy boots hunting? (Explained)

Texas Walkabout
March 16, 2022
Updated on March 16, 2022

Should you wear cowboy boots while hunting?

If you buy the correct brand and model, yes. Many pairs of cowboy boots are not suited for hunting; however, there are some models that are. Examples include Thorogood Men's Snake Boots and Ariat Men's Groundbreaker Square toe Word Boot. There are also Rocky Men's Lynx Waterproof Snake Hunting Boot and HISEA Apollo Pro 400G Insulated Men's Hunting Boots. See, you do have options!

 

Are cowboy boots good for hunting?

In the past, there were not any options for this. Today they make cowboy boots for multiple purposes. It is possible to buy them in a style and look that are attractive while still using them for an activity such as hunting. Nowadays, there are choices.

Keep in mind that, when you go hunting, it typically involves hiking through rocky, wooded terrain. If you were dressy cowboy boots, you will not get around so well. You need a brand and model of boot that is better for hunting trips.

 

Are leather boots OK for hunting?

They are if you buy the right ones. If you buy a stylish-looking pair of leather cowboy boots with narrow toe area and a higher heel, do not attempt to go hunting in those. If you ignore our advice, you will be miserable. This style of boot is not good for things like that.

As we said, in today's boot market, there are varieties of leather cowboy boots that are fine for hunting or other outdoor activities. Expect to have to clean these boots after every hunting trip or other outdoor event. Otherwise, dirt and mud will get caked up on the boots and ruin them.

 

Is there a difference amongst boot types?

In reality, yes. There are boots make for formal events and those made for night-life. There are some made for hiking and some made for hunting. Then you can find boots suitable to work in. Cowboy boot makers have adjusted to this though. More and more makers are creating models that are multi-functional.

These specific boot crafters recognized the need for multipurpose cowboy boots and set out to accommodate this need. We would say they have succeeded. There are probably hundreds of versions on the market today. You will discover some that you can use for an activity such as hunting and not worry. Make sure you try them on before buying them to ensure they are comfortable.

 

Why would you wear cowboy boots while hunting?

There are some people, both men and women, who insist on wearing cowboy boots for every occasion. They are usually boot aficionados who know which kind to buy for which purpose. These folks will always find just the right pair of boots.

While sites like ours can make suggestions, it is really up to the individual to decide what to buy. If they choose to wear cowboy boots hunting, who is going to stop them. There really is no such thing as fashion police. Who cares what other people think anyhow. If you want to do this, go right ahead!

 

What are snake-proof cowboy boots?

In reality, there is no such thing. While leather cowboy boots will protect the bottom of your feet from snake bites, they do not from bites on the top and side of your feet. If you are within prime striking distance of a snake, it is possible for the bite to pierce the leather. Truth be told, you are best off wearing a boot with a tougher material that is better for evading snakes when you go hunting. Save your cowboy boots for other uses.

 

Can women wear cowboy boots when hunting?

Really, the same answer applies as it does to men. She should select pairs specifically geared towards rugged activity. She will want to visit a store that focuses on the many styles of cowboy boots. This is her best opportunity to find what she needs.

 

Advice for men and women about this topic

As we mentioned earlier, it is important to go to a physical store to look for these. You can tell the salesperson exactly what you want and try each pair on until you strike gold. If you intend to go hunting often, perhaps by more than one pair as long as you are at the store. A person cannot own too many pairs of cowboy boots!

 

Final thoughts

In the end, it is your feet we are talking about. If you really want to wear cowboy boots on hunting trips, we aren't going to tell you not to. In this case, do not expect the boots to last very long though. They will undergo a lot of punishment on hunting and hiking trips. It is also your money. If you want to shell it out for scores of pairs of boots, by all means do it!
