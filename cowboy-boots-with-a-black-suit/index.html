Cowboy boots with a black suit (Explained)

Cowboy Boots Texas Walkabout 03.16.2022
(Updated on 03.16.2022 )

Can you wear cowboy boots with a black suit?

Yes! In fact, you can wear cowboy boots with any color suit. Years ago the answer would have been no. However, the times have changed and so have fashion trends. The key in any of these instances is to pick the appropriate color. Want more information? Then keep reading this article.

Your cowboy boot collection

Ideally, you should have a large collection of cowboy boots. It ought to contain one of every major color and style. This is so you have an appropriate pair to wear with every color suit as well as with almost any other items of clothing and for the appropriate occasion.

So, what would happen if I ignored these rules?

In reality, nothing! Fashion rules are merely suggestions. It is not like the fashion police are going to arrest you for wearing other combinations. You might get laughed at, but who cares, right? This is still a free country, wear whatever you want! Enjoy your boots!

Should you wear cowboy boots with a black suit?

As we said, nowadays, cowboy boots can be worn with just about anything including a black suit. In the past, they were not considered formal enough to wear with such an outfit. Today, with the hundreds of colors and styles on the market, it is possible to own the perfect pair to go with your black suit or any other color suit.

More thoughts on this topic

Picture this! You in your black suit with a freshly-shined pair of black, tan, or brown dressy cowboy boots. My, how handsome you will look. You will be the envy of most other men.

What would happen if my boots were not shined?

You would look ridiculous! It would ruin the entire outfit. You usually know when you are going to need to wear your cowboy boots. Therefore, you have more than enough time to shine them and get them ready, not to mention to choose the best color for the suit or dress. Shine them!

Brown Cowboy boots with black suit

Some so-called fashion gurus frown upon this combination. We disagree! We think it is a stunning combination, especially if the brown cowboy boots include a spiffy design. Think about how handsome that is! Try adding a brown tie and accessories to compliment those brown boots. Marvelous!

Can women wear cowboy boots with dresses?

In today's times, yes she can. Again, she should select the appropriate color and style to go with the dress she is wearing. She also needs to make sure her boots are clean and shiny. Cowboy boot polish is for women to use as well as men.

Can a man wear cowboy boots with a tuxedo?

Yes you can, as long as you choose a pair that is equally-elegant in style. Since you would wear a tux to a very formal occasion, your cowboy boots need to be just as formal. Pick a pair with an intricate stitching pattern and a suitable color to go with your tux and other accessories.

What should you NEVER wear with cowboy boots?

Even in 2022, there are still some items of clothing you should not wear with cowboy boots. Please note: some people say you cannot wear with fringe and sequins with cowboy boots. We beg to differ. If you have ever seen people in parades with such outfits, they proudly wear cowboy boots. They look absolutely amazing. Therefore, we say go ahead and wear this combination! So, what are the thing you shouldn't wear with them? Here is our list.

  • Overalls
  • Prairie dresses
  • Athletic-styled clothes
  • Too many accessories, instead pick one or two nice pieces
  • Wedding dresses and bridal party dresses
  • Sportswear
  • Lace dresses or skirts

Final Thoughts

Once again, the points we have illustrated here are really suggestions even though fashion experts deem them to be rules. In the end, it is up to you to decide what to wear. Who knows, maybe you will be the person to start a new fashion trend. Wear your cowboy boots and be happy!
